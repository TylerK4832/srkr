About South River Kayak Rental

Our local company was born in Edgewater MD, on the banks of the South River. We are independently owned and operated, and we strive to give the lowest prices and largest range of selection, so that our customers are able to easily enjoy all that the South River has to offer.

South River Kayak Rental is open 7 days a week from April to October. We offer modes of aquatic transportation to fit any need: you can choose from a variety of kayaks, paddleboards or canoes. Rent for an hour, a day, or up to one week. We also offer delivery to your house or local marina!
